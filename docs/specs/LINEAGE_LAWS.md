# .NeoN Lineage Laws

These laws define invariant behavior for `.neon` artifacts.

## Law 1 — Origin Persistence

Every artifact must preserve a reference to its origin context.

## Law 2 — Derivation Visibility

Derived artifacts must declare ancestry.

## Law 3 — Proof Portability

Proof packets must remain verifiable after transport.

## Law 4 — Human Readability

A `.neon` artifact must remain understandable without proprietary software.

## Law 5 — Local Sovereignty

The creator's local vault is the primary source of private truth.

## Law 6 — Minimal Exposure

Public anchors should expose commitments, not secrets.

## Law 7 — Lineage Continuity

Lineage should not silently disappear during transformation.

## Law 8 — Agnostic Durability

Artifacts should survive changes in models, chains, vendors, clouds, and interfaces.

## Law 9 — Attribution Continuity

Attribution survives automation.

## Law 10 — Composable Simplicity

Prefer small composable primitives over monolithic systems.
