# .NeoN Glossary

## .NeoN

A protocol project for persistent human-origin intelligence lineage.

## .N

The minimal mark for a traceable .NeoN object.

## Human-Origin Intelligence

Reusable intelligence that begins with a human contribution, including ideas, workflows, prompts, automations, processes, systems, or operational logic.

## Artifact

A durable unit of human-origin intelligence that can be recorded, versioned, signed, searched, exported, and referenced.

## Commit

A recorded change to an artifact.

## Ledger

An append-only record of artifact events.

## Lineage

The chain of origin, change, reuse, influence, and derivation attached to an artifact.

## Proof Packet

A portable bundle containing enough evidence to support an origin or contribution claim.

## Anchor

A minimal public timestamp or hash commitment used to prove that a claim existed at a time.

## Vault

A creator-owned local store for artifacts, ledger entries, proofs, indexes, and identity records.

## Impact Record

A claim or measurement describing value created by an artifact.

## Confidence Tier

A rating that describes the strength of proof behind a claim.

## Design Law

The creator carries the lineage, not the platform.
