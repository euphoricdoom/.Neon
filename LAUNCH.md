# .NeoN Launch Declaration

.NeoN is a public protocol project for human-origin intelligence provenance.

## One Line

.NeoN is the Git of human-origin intelligence ownership.

## Origin

Founded by Carl Sowers.

Built from the belief that automation should preserve the human origin of the systems it scales.

## What We Are Building

A creator-first infrastructure layer for:

- workflows
- automations
- prompts
- agent chains
- process systems
- operational knowledge
- reusable intelligence artifacts

## The Mission

Make human-created intelligence visible, portable, versioned, measurable, and attributable.

## The First Standard

Attribution survives automation.

## The First Product Direction

WorkLedger: a local-first proof system for creators to register artifacts, timestamps, hashes, lineage, and impact records.

## The First Symbol

.N

A small mark for traceable human-origin intelligence.

## The Promise

Creators should be able to prove what they built.
Creators should be able to carry their contribution history.
Creators should not disappear when their systems scale.
